Welcome to the Online Experiment

Instruction:

The online experiment includes four steps

Step 1. Register

On the next page, you need to register the online experiment, you can either use an email address or a random number to register.

Step 2. A questionnaire

After registering the experiment, you will at first fill out a short questionnaire.

Step 3. Three tasks

You will be asked to process one task. The task is about selecting appropriate design techniques for different design situations. Design techniques mean following a specific procedure to conduct design activities.


After each task, there will be a short questionnaire.
