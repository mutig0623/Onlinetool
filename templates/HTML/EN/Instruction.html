Welcome to the Online Experiment



Instruction:

The online experiment includes three steps,which will take around 15 minutes.

Step 1. Register

On the next page, you need to register the online experiment, you can either use an email address or a random number to register.

Step 2. A questionnaire

After registering the experiment, you will at first fill out a short questionnaire.

Step 3. A task + a questionnair

You will be asked to process a task. The task is about selecting appropriate design techniques for different design situations. Design techniques mean following a specific procedure to conduct design activities.
